Online Shopping System

Project Overview Description: The Online Shopping System is a comprehensive e-commerce platform designed to provide users with a seamless and secure shopping experience. This project was developed to cater to the growing trend of online shopping, enabling users to browse through a wide range of products, add them to their cart, and complete the purchase through a secure checkout process.
Key Features
Product Catalog: Displayed a diverse range of products with detailed information, including images, descriptions, and prices. Implemented product categorization and filtering for easy navigation.
User Authentication: Implemented a secure user authentication system, allowing users to register, log in, and manage their profiles.
Shopping Cart: Enabled users to add products to their cart, update quantities, and remove items. Provided a real-time view of the shopping cart with the total cost.
Checkout Process: Streamlined the checkout process with a user-friendly interface. Integrated a secure payment gateway for transactions.
Order Management: Implemented an order management system for users to view their order history and track order status.
User Reviews and Ratings: Allowed users to leave reviews and ratings for products, enhancing the shopping experience.
Responsive Design: Ensured a responsive design for optimal user experience across various devices, including desktops, tablets, and smartphones.
